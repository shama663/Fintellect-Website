Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start trading?

Sign up, add funds to your account, and go to the Start Trading page to buy stocks.

How do I track my stocks?

Your purchased stocks will appear in the Portfolio section under the Account page.

Is there a minimum investment?

No, you can start with any amount, but ensure you have sufficient funds to cover stock prices.
